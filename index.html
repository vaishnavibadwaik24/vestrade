Benefits of Large scale Projects

Cost Advantages of Larger Land Parcels

Buying large parcels of land often comes at a lower price per unit compared to smaller plots, ensuring an additional profit margin for developers.

Market Dynamics: Small vs. Large Land Parcels

Small land parcels often face high demand and competition among buyers, driving up their prices. In contrast, large land parcels have fewer buyers, offering developers better negotiation opportunities and lower per-unit costs.

Optimizing Development Costs in Large Projects

Expenses like sample units, site offices, marketing, and staff salaries remain constant across both small and large projects, but in larger projects, these costs are spread over more units, reducing the per-unit cost.

Enhanced Infrastructure and Project Value

Developers can plan and execute infrastructure projects like roads, utilities, and green spaces while incorporating multiple amenities, lowering development costs and increasing project value.
